The team at numberresearch.xyz has been doing incredible work documenting all positive integers. To complement their research, we founded Negative Number Research Inc. — taking on the other half of the number line.

At Negative Number Research Inc., we are attempting to find and document all* available negative numbers.

This is a volunteer-lead research position, where anyone is able to contribute. Simply type a negative number in, and we'll check if we've got it. If we have, no worries, just try another. If it's a new number, thank you for your hard work!

*Currently, we are focusing our research on negative integers. Once this has been completed, then we will be able to look into positives and decimals.
